# Sponsor features

The Front Matter AI feature has been discontinued as of version `10.10.0`.

The `frontMatter.sponsors.ai.enabled` setting and all related AI suggestion functionality have been removed.

For support and to contribute to Front Matter CMS, you can still become a sponsor by visiting the [GitHub sponsor page](https://github.com/sponsors/estruyf).
